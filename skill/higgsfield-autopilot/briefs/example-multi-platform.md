# Brief: Q3 Brand Hero — Multi-Platform

> **Pattern:** multi-platform-render
> **Aspects:** 9:16, 1:1, 16:9
> **Format:** still

## Concept

A single hero image for our Q3 brand campaign: a young architect standing on a mass-timber rooftop terrace at golden hour, reviewing physical blueprints. Background shows a soft skyline of low-rise sustainable buildings under construction. Warm late-afternoon light, hopeful but grounded mood. Editorial style — think New York Times Magazine cover, not stock photography.

Render natively in each aspect (don't crop one). We need:
- 9:16 for IG Reels cover + LinkedIn vertical post
- 1:1 for IG feed + Twitter
- 16:9 for the website hero + YouTube channel banner

## Notes

- No identifiable brand logos visible in any shot
- Architect should not be looking at camera — we want a candid-feeling moment
- Avoid AI-photography tells: weird hands on the blueprints, extra fingers, six-fingered architect
